..About Me..

I have been fortunate to find two things I love to do passionately. My life before this, I worked in Pharmacy. I had reached master status in the world of compounding intravenous medications. Antibiotics, Chemotherapy, pain-management for adults, children and babies. Working for a large local hospital in the inpatient pharmacy. I learned to handle stress well, I was apart of the code team. If a patient in the hospital went into cardiac arrest, they would page us over head. It was our job to rush to the room and take over. The stress is intense, having the patient’s family standing just out of reach as I compounded life saving drugs out of a tackle box on the floor. I have the skills acquired from working 12 years in a large hospital. working as a team in every day tasks and in high stress moments. I worked for just as long at a Home Infusion Pharmacy. Training new staff, new technicians. I came to realize that I had reached the top of that life. There was no more challenge, nothing to keep my brain happy. So, I decided to take a step in a different direction

a father of three children, well soon to be three teenagers, I could not justify making a carrier switch at the risk of not enjoying it. So, I started teaching myself the basics of coding. It was in this moment that I realized that this was it. This is where I needed to be. It would be an understatement to say that my life is driven by my curiosity. I love taking everything around me apart, just to see how it works. Once its secrets where known I would toss it off to the side and look for the next thing. I was completely consumed with this world of coding. It’s a never-ending puzzle with an endless stream of excitement. I have learned a lot so far, but its knowing that there is a lifetime of more learning, that’ when I smile the most. I am a carrier changer, I’m not a young 20-year-o
